# Client Enumerations Resource

## Level Object Data
The following enumerations are used by [level objects](level-object.md) as possible values for parameters.

### Easing
The easing function used by most motion triggers (Move, Rotate, Scale, etc)

| Key | Name/Value                           
|:----|:---------------------------
| 0   | None
| 1   | Ease In Out
| 2   | Ease In
| 3   | Ease Out
| 4   | Elastic In Out
| 5   | Elastic In
| 6   | Elastic Out
| 7   | Bounce In Out
| 8   | Bounce In
| 9   | Bounce Out
| 10  | Exponential In Out
| 11  | Exponential In
| 12  | Exponential Out
| 13  | Sine In Out
| 14  | Sine In
| 15  | Sine Out
| 16  | Back In Out
| 17  | Back In
| 18  | Back Out
